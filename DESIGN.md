# Design System: D-Shirts by Daniel (Dark Mode Edition)

This document defines the visual identity for the "Dark Mode" edition of D-Shirts.

## 1. Brand Identity
- **Vibe:** "luxury streetwear", "dark system", "cyber/industrial".
- **Feel:** Serious, high-contrast, minimal, floating.
- **Mascot:** (Still the Lemon/Squirrel, but presented in a dark context).

## 2. Color Palette

| Usage | Color | Hex | Notes |
|-------|-------|-----|-------|
| **Background** | **Solid Black** | `#000000` | STRICT. No white backgrounds. |
| **Surface** | **Dark Grey** | `#111111` | For cards or subtle overlays. |
| **Text (Primary)** | **White** | `#FFFFFF` | High contrast headers. |
| **Text (Secondary)** | **Grey** | `#888888` | Prices or metadata. |
| **Accents** | **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Borders, buttons. |

## 3. Typography

### Headings
- **Style:** Sans-serif, Uppercase, Wide Spacing.
- **Font:** Inter, Roboto, or System Sans.
- **Spacing:** `tracking-widest` (3px+).

### Body
- **Style:** Minimal, clean.
- **Color:** `#CCCCCC`.

## 4. Components

### Visual Layering (Hero)
- **Layer 1 (Back):** Massive Logo, low opacity (0.3), blurred (`blur(5px)`).
- **Layer 2 (Front):** Sharp, centered content. Drop shadow.

### Buttons
- **Style:** Ghost button.
- **Border:** 1px solid White.
- **Text:** Uppercase White.
- **Hover:** Background White, Text Black.

### Product Cards
- **Background:** Transparent or `#111`.
- **Effect:** Floating (drop shadow).
- **Action:** "BUY ON ETSY" (External Link).

## 5. Layout
- **Hero:** Full status screen.
- **Grid:** Floating cards on black void.
